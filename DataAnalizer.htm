DataAnalizer

Analyze CSV/text datasets quickly in-browser — summary, numeric stats, frequencies.
Model: GPT-5 mini (copilot)
Description

This tool analyzes pasted or uploaded data, computes column summaries, numeric statistics, and top values. Follow the steps below to prepare and analyze data.

  1. Define the question or goal for your dataset.
  2. Paste CSV or upload a CSV file below (delimiters supported: comma/semicolon/tab).
  3. Click Analyze to get column summaries and quick insights.
Tip: If your file uses a different delimiter, choose it above. For single-file apps, this runs fully in your browser.
